# L4 - SEOs , Core web vitals

SEO - Search Engine Optimization - Content that loads faster will be given higher preference by the browser
Core web vitals - When similar performance and similar content , then core vitals are checked
       -  CLS(Cumulative Layout Shift) - How much shift of content does the page experience while loading (should be as less as possible)
                                         eg- some content loads first , user tries to click it but just prior to click
                                             New content loaded and the page content shifted left or right or up or down and user clicked the 
                                             incorrect place . 
       -  LCP(Largest Contextual Paint) - how much time does the largest element of the page takes to load (ideally below 2.5s)
       -  FID(First Input Delay) - time of processing the first user input in a page(100ms or less ideally)

       and more...

Meta Tags --- description and all , Earlier were used extensively but not anymore , in times of AI , pages are valued and evaluated by parsing instead of meta tags
